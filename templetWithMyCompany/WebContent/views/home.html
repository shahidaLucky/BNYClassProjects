Global Well


Global Well is a multifaceted non-profit organization that is still in its growth phase. Global Well is currently running operations in 25 countries, with more than 200 different projects and programs over the world. This company currently has over 120,000 employees over the world and 8.39% of total employees are composed from U.S. workforce. Amongst the U.S. workforce 42% of them are deployed to international programs. The current turnover rate of U.S. citizens in line staff is 11% and 8% amongst supervisory staff. The international programs turnover rate for line staff is 8% and supervisory staff is 4.5%. Global Well U.S. citizen positions are currently 13% understaffed in line staff and 9.5% understaffed in supervisory staff and in international programs they are understaffed by 9% in line staff and 7% in supervisory staff.
